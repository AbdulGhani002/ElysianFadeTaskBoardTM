# User Manual

## Introduction

Welcome to the Elysian Fade Task BoardTM User Manual. This guide will help you understand how to use the software effectively. The Elysian Fade Task BoardTM is a feature-rich task management system designed to help you manage tasks, subtasks, tags, users, teams, notifications, goals, and analytics.

## Quick Start Guide

### Setup

1. **Clone the repository**:
   ```sh
   git clone https://github.com/yourusername/ElysianFadeTaskBoardTM.git
   cd ElysianFadeTaskBoardTM
   ```

2. **Install dependencies**:
   ```sh
   npm install
   ```

3. **Set up environment variables**:
   Create a `.env` file in the root directory and add the following:
   ```
   MONGODB_URI=your_mongodb_uri
   JWT_SECRET=your_jwt_secret
   PORT=3000
   ```

4. **Start the application**:
   ```sh
   npm start
   ```

### Accessing the Application

- Open your web browser and navigate to `http://localhost:3000`.

## User Interface

### Dashboard

The dashboard is the main interface where you can manage tasks, subtasks, tags, users, teams, notifications, goals, and analytics. It provides an overview of your tasks and allows you to perform various actions.

### Task Management

- **Create Task**: Click on the "Create Task" button to open the task creation modal. Fill in the task details and click "Save" to create the task.
- **Edit Task**: Click on a task to open the task details modal. Make the necessary changes and click "Save" to update the task.
- **Delete Task**: Click on the delete icon next to a task to delete it.

### Subtask Management

- **Add Subtask**: Open a task and click on the "Add Subtask" button. Fill in the subtask details and click "Save" to add the subtask.
- **Edit Subtask**: Click on a subtask to open the subtask details modal. Make the necessary changes and click "Save" to update the subtask.
- **Delete Subtask**: Click on the delete icon next to a subtask to delete it.

### Tag Management

- **Create Tag**: Click on the "Create Tag" button to open the tag creation modal. Fill in the tag details and click "Save" to create the tag.
- **Assign Tag to Task**: Open a task and click on the "Assign Tag" button. Select the tag from the list and click "Save" to assign the tag to the task.
- **Remove Tag from Task**: Open a task and click on the "Remove Tag" button next to the tag you want to remove.

### User Management

- **Register User**: Click on the "Register" button to open the registration form. Fill in the user details and click "Register" to create the user.
- **Login User**: Click on the "Login" button to open the login form. Enter your email and password and click "Login" to access the application.
- **Update Profile**: Click on your profile icon and select "Update Profile". Make the necessary changes and click "Save" to update your profile.

### Team Management

- **Create Team**: Click on the "Create Team" button to open the team creation modal. Fill in the team details and click "Save" to create the team.
- **Add Member to Team**: Open a team and click on the "Add Member" button. Select the user from the list and click "Save" to add the member to the team.
- **Remove Member from Team**: Open a team and click on the "Remove Member" button next to the member you want to remove.
- **Assign Role to Member**: Open a team and click on the "Assign Role" button next to the member. Select the role from the list and click "Save" to assign the role to the member.

### Notification Management

- **Send Task Reminder**: Open a task and click on the "Send Reminder" button. Select the user from the list and click "Send" to send the task reminder.
- **Send Deadline Notification**: Open a task and click on the "Send Deadline Notification" button. Select the user from the list and click "Send" to send the deadline notification.
- **Notify Team**: Open a team and click on the "Notify Team" button. Enter the message and click "Send" to notify the team.

### Goal Management

- **Create Goal**: Click on the "Create Goal" button to open the goal creation modal. Fill in the goal details and click "Save" to create the goal.
- **Edit Goal**: Click on a goal to open the goal details modal. Make the necessary changes and click "Save" to update the goal.
- **Delete Goal**: Click on the delete icon next to a goal to delete it.

### Analytics

- **Generate Daily Report**: Click on the "Generate Daily Report" button to generate the daily report.
- **Generate Weekly Summary**: Click on the "Generate Weekly Summary" button to generate the weekly summary.
- **Generate Team Performance Report**: Click on the "Generate Team Performance Report" button to generate the team performance report.

## FAQs

### How do I reset my password?

To reset your password, click on the "Forgot Password" link on the login page. Enter your email address and follow the instructions to reset your password.

### How do I change my email address?

To change your email address, go to your profile settings and update your email address. Click "Save" to apply the changes.

### How do I delete my account?

To delete your account, go to your profile settings and click on the "Delete Account" button. Confirm the action to delete your account.

## Tutorials

### Creating a Task

1. Click on the "Create Task" button on the dashboard.
2. Fill in the task details, including title, description, due date, priority, status, category, and assignees.
3. Click "Save" to create the task.

### Assigning a Tag to a Task

1. Open a task by clicking on it.
2. Click on the "Assign Tag" button.
3. Select the tag from the list and click "Save" to assign the tag to the task.

### Adding a Subtask

1. Open a task by clicking on it.
2. Click on the "Add Subtask" button.
3. Fill in the subtask details, including title and status.
4. Click "Save" to add the subtask.

### Generating a Daily Report

1. Click on the "Analytics" tab on the dashboard.
2. Click on the "Generate Daily Report" button.
3. View the generated daily report.
